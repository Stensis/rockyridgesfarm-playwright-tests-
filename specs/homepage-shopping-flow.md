# Homepage and Shopping Flow Test Plan

## Goal
Verify that users can navigate Rocky Ridges Farm, browse products, add items to cart, and reach checkout.

## Test 1: Homepage loads
Steps:
1. Open https://www.rockyridgesfarm.com/
2. Verify the homepage loads successfully.
3. Verify the main heading "Fresh from Rocky Ridges Farm" is visible.

Expected result:
- Homepage opens without errors.
- Main heading is visible.

## Test 2: Navigation links work
Steps:
1. Open the homepage.
2. Click Shop.
3. Click About.
4. Click Contact.
5. Click Home.

Expected result:
- Each navigation link opens the correct page.

## Test 3: Browse product categories
Steps:
1. Open the Shop page.
2. Click Fresh Vegetables.
3. Click Seasonal Fruits.
4. Click Tubers & Roots.
5. Click Herbs & Spices.

Expected result:
- Each category displays matching products.

## Test 4: Add products to cart
Steps:
1. Open the Shop page.
2. Add Fresh Carrots to cart.
3. Add Apples to cart.
4. Add Avocados to cart.
5. Open the cart.

Expected result:
- Added products appear in the cart.

## Test 5: Update cart
Steps:
1. Open the cart.
2. Remove one item.
3. Increase quantity of one item.

Expected result:
- Cart updates correctly.

## Test 6: Checkout requires login
Steps:
1. Open the cart.
2. Click Proceed to Checkout.
3. Click Log In.

Expected result:
- User is asked to log in before checkout.

## Test 7: Help and policy pages work
Steps:
1. Open FAQ.
2. Expand "What areas do you deliver to?"
3. Open Terms & Conditions.
4. Open Privacy Policy.
5. Open Refunds & Returns.
6. Open Delivery Policy.

Expected result:
- FAQ expands correctly.
- Policy pages open successfully.
